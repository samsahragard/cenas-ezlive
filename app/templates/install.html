Install the dashboard

on your phone home screen
app.cenaskitchen.com

🍎iPhone / iPad (Safari)

  1. Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari.
  2. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  3. Tap Add in the top-right.
  4. Cenas Kitchen now opens like a regular app from your home screen.

🤖Android (Chrome)

  1. Tap the three-dot menu ⋮ in the top right of Chrome.
  2. Tap Install app or Add to Home screen.
  3. Confirm with Install.
  4. Open Cenas Kitchen from your home screen — it'll act like a native app.

💻Desktop (Chrome / Edge)

  1. Open app.cenaskitchen.com.
  2. Click the install icon (⊕) on the right side of the address bar.
  3. Click Install in the prompt.

After installing, open Cenas Kitchen from your home screen and sign in with the passcode you've been given.
