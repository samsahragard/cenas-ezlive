Rules: $25 per delivery (always) · $10 tracked bonus + $2.00 per verified mile over 20 + $5 for a 5★, all gated on tracking_status='Tracked'. The three miles columns are all "extra over 20", one-way from the home kitchen to the first drop-off (Google Routes API): E-Miles is expected (from the route), D-Miles is what the driver actually drove (from ezCater route history unless a manager overrides it), and V-Miles is what a manager verifies on the Ez Drivers page — V-Miles is what pays. Until a manager verifies an order, V-Miles defaults to the expected miles, so pay is unchanged. $10, Notes, and the 5★ bonus are manager inputs from the Ez Drivers page.
