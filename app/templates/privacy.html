Privacy Policy

Cenas Kitchen Employee · Last updated: 2026-05-12

This Privacy Policy describes how Cenas Kitchen, LLC ("we", "our", "Cenas Kitchen") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Cenas Kitchen Employee application and the related website at app.cenaskitchen.com (collectively, the "Service"). The Service is provided to employees, contractors, and authorized partners of Cenas Kitchen for internal restaurant operations only. It is not intended for the general public.

1. Information we collect

We collect only information needed to run restaurant operations and pay our team. Specifically:

  • Account info — your full name, work email, work phone number, address, role, and home location (Tomball or Copperfield). Provided when an authorized manager adds your account.
  • Authentication credentials — a 5-digit passcode you create. Stored only as a one-way salted hash; we never see your plaintext passcode. Used to sign in to the Service.
  • Shift & payroll data — work hours, breaks, roles, and computed pay totals. Read from our Toast POS integration for shifts you actually worked.
  • Driver GPS location — only if your role is "Corporate Driver". While you have an active delivery shift open, the app captures location every ~5 seconds and uploads it to our servers. Used to provide live delivery tracking to the dispatch manager and to compute mileage-based pay for routes over 20 miles one-way. Location is NOT captured outside of an active shift, and is NEVER captured for non-driver roles.
  • Delivery performance data — for drivers, per- delivery records including on-time status, tracked status, five-star reviews, and verified mileage. Used to compute delivery bonuses and the per-mile-over-20 pay tier.
  • App diagnostics — basic crash logs, error messages, and request timing. No screen content, no keystrokes, no microphone.

2. How we use the information

  • To sign you in and route you to the right dashboard for your role.
  • To compute your paycheck (base per-delivery rate, on-time bonus, mileage tier, five-star bonus per the published ezCater Guidelines).
  • To dispatch and monitor in-progress deliveries (live driver location map for active shifts).
  • To audit restaurant operations and labor cost.
  • To respond to your questions or service requests.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not use the data for advertising. We do not share it with third parties except as described in §3.

3. Third-party processors

We use a small number of vendors to run the Service. Each receives only the minimum information necessary:

  • Render — application hosting (our servers run on Render's infrastructure).
  • Toast, Inc. — point-of-sale data, time entries, and payroll inputs.
  • Sling (Toolbox by Toast) — scheduling and roster data.
  • ezCater, Inc. — catering order data for deliveries dispatched through their platform.
  • Google Maps / OpenStreetMap — distance lookups for route planning and live driver map rendering.
  • Anthropic — AI-assisted PDF extraction for catering orders and AI-assisted resolution of ingest warnings.
  • SiteGround — email transport for order notifications.

4. How we protect your information

  • All connections to the Service use TLS encryption.
  • Passcodes are stored as one-way salted hashes (Werkzeug pbkdf2:sha256, the OWASP-recommended pattern).
  • Roles are enforced server-side. Only Partner-level accounts can see management labor costs; only your own GMs or Sam can reset your passcode.
  • Driver location data is retained for 90 days for payroll audit, then deleted.
  • If a passcode is reset or your account is deactivated, all your sessions on every device are immediately invalidated.

5. Your choices and rights

  • Access & correction — ask your manager or email samsahragard@gmail.com to view, correct, or update your profile information.
  • Deletion — request that we remove your account and personal records by emailing the same address. We will remove your account within 30 days. Note that some data may be retained as required by tax or payroll law (typically 4 years for payroll records in Texas).
  • Stop tracking — drivers can revoke the location permission in their phone's Settings → Apps → Cenas Kitchen Employee → Permissions, or simply end their shift in the app. Location is never captured outside of an active shift.

6. Children

The Service is intended for use by Cenas Kitchen employees and authorized adult partners only. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change. Material changes will also be announced via the app's developer chat for authorized staff.
