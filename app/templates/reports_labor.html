Notes

  • Net sales = sum of check.amount (pre-tax subtotal) across non-voided checks. Excludes tax, tips, and voided checks.
  • % Net Sales = position labor cost / net sales × 100. Industry-standard labor cost ratio.
  • % of Labor = position labor cost / total labor cost × 100. (How payroll is distributed within the labor pool.)
  • Hours = regularHours + overtimeHours (clocked time, not scheduled).
  • Labor cost = regularHours × hourlyWage + overtimeHours × hourlyWage × 1.5 (1.5× OT multiplier).
  • Tips (cash + CC) are server earnings paid by customer, NOT employer expense — not in labor cost.
  • Salaried positions (GM, Owner) show $0 in Toast hourlyWage — their real cost lives in payroll.
  • Deleted time entries + voided checks excluded.
