How this works

  • Each Monday-ish, Alvarado and J. Luna email their weekly price sheet to orders@cenaskitchen.com. The IMAP poller (running on Render) parses each sheet and writes one snapshot row per item to the produce_price_snapshot table.
  • "Biggest movers" compares each item's most recent two snapshots per vendor and surfaces anything that moved ≥ {{ "%.0f"|format(movers_threshold) }}%. Negative = price went down (good for us); positive = vendor bumped the price.
  • Per-item chart shows both vendors over the chosen lookback window. Easy to screenshot and send back when a vendor explains a sudden jump.
  • Currently the page shows org-wide pricing — vendor sheets aren't location-specific. Per-store URL prefix is preserved for navigation symmetry.
  • Also see Produce Order Guide for placing a new order using these prices.
