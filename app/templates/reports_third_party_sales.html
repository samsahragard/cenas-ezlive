Notes

  • Source: Toast. Each Toast order has a source field (In Store / API / Online / etc.). We exclude In Store and group the rest by channel.
  • DoorDash detection: orders with source = "API" + deliveryInfo.address1 starting with "DoorDash" (DoorDash uses a placeholder HQ address since they don't share customer addresses with restaurants).
  • Other third-parties: when Uber Eats / Grubhub get integrated to Toast, they'll auto-appear here using the same address-placeholder detection.
  • Sales = sum of check.amount (pre-tax, pre-tip) across non-voided checks. Same denominator as the Labor report's net sales.
  • Avg ticket = sales / orders for that channel.
  • If a channel shows lower volume than expected, the third-party integration may be partial — verify with Toast that all the platform's orders are routing through the integration. (DoorDash on Cenas Kitchen was just enabled — volume will grow.)
